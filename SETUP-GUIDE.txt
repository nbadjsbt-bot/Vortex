*Note: This funnel has been implemented as a modern React application for supreme speed, animations, and conversion optimization.*

1. CONFIGURATION
Open `src/config.ts` to edit your affiliate link, colors, images, and author bio. This replaces the traditional WordPress Customizer with a much faster, code-based config file that ensures peak performance.

2. ADDING YOUR AFFILIATE LINK
In `src/config.ts`, replace the `affiliateLink` value with your actual Digistore24 affiliate link (preferably cloaked with a Pretty Link).

3. UPLOADING YOUR PHOTO
Upload your author photo to any image host (or just place it in the `/public` folder of this project), and update the `author.photo` URL in `src/config.ts`.

4. DEPLOYMENT (Vercel, Netlify, Cloudflare)
Since this is a React SPA, you can deploy it for FREE on Netlify or Vercel. 
- Run `npm run build`
- Deploy the `dist` folder. 
It takes 30 seconds and requires zero hosting fees.

5. EMAILS
The 7-part email sequence is provided in `EMAILS.txt`. Load these into your autoresponder (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, etc.). Ensure your opt-in form connects to your autoresponder list, and set the success redirect of your form to point to `/bridge`.
